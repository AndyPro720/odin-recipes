    Steps

  1. Clean the boneless chicken(500g), if thigh pieces make incisions to allow for better cooking. If breast pieces make butterfly slice them from the middle along with the incisions.
  2. Pound the chicken or hammer the chicken with the spine of the knife to break down the muscle fiber, make sure the chicken is intact as a whole piece, pound all the chicken pieces in same way.
  3. Set a wok on medium high heat, add oil(1-2 tbsp), whole spices( Jeera, tej patta(2), elachi(3-4)) & sliced onions, cook until the onions are translucent. Add some ginger garlic paste to taste.
