Description

It was one of those I instantly fell in love with, although took me close to an year to upload it up here! Credits go to Mrs Limaye for introducing me to it!

    Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Curd/ Dahi
  • Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Spring/green Onions
  • Mustard
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bell Peppers
  • baby corn
  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Olive Oil
  • Pomegranate

    Steps

  1. Boil pasta(~250g) with some salt and oil to a full cook.(rather than al-dente)
  2. Strain and set aside the pasta aside to cool down. Sprinkle some olive oil and mix to avoid sticking.
  3. Finely chop onions(1-2), bell peppers(1-2), corn(3/4 cup), carrot, cucumber.(1)
  4. Peel and set aside 1/2 a cup pomegranate(seeds).
  5. Put the pasta in a large mixing bowl.
  6. Add some curd(1/4 cup), mayo, honey(~3 tbsp) and some mustard.(or powdered seeds)
  7. Give it a good whirl and toss in the veggies giving it good mix.
  8. Season
  9. Adjust the sauces and set aside for at least 15-20 mins in the fridge to cool down.
  10. Serve Chilled
