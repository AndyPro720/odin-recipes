Description

A no bullshit, simple and yet authentic pasta arrabbiata. Arrabbiata roughly translates to angry in Italian so we make this hot by default, one can alter the spices to their liking.

    Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Chili
  • Chili Flakes
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

    Steps

  1. Boil some water.
  2. Around 4-5 tomatoes for around 10-15 seconds for blanching.
  3. Dip them back in cold water and peel their skin off.
  4. Finely chop the tomatoes.
  5. Mince some garlic.
  6. If using red-chili, deseed and mince finely.
  7. One can completely omit this step and use chili flakes instead too.
